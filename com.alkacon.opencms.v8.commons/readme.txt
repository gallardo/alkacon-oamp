compatible with OpenCms 8.0.1 or later.

3. Installation

In the OpenCms workplace (Administration view) click on "Module Administration".
Click on "Import Module with HTTP". 
Select the file "com.alkacon.opencms.v8.commons_1.0.0.zip" and click OK.
Restart your servlet container.
